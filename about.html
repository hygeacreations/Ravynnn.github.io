What is The Raven's Perch?

The Raven's Perch is a discord server, established on 30th October 2018, specifically designed to help people on their journey to practising Witchcraft. If you also want to learn about it but not practice it then this is the place for you. In The Raven's Perch, we have many features such as:
● Self Assignable Roles
● Suggestion System
● Happy Thoughts channel where you can share happy thoughts to spread positive energies
● Classes on different magickal topics every month
● 18+ channels (for verified 18+)
